## 💡 PRO TIPS

### Reddit/Quora
- **90/10 rule:** 90% value, 10% pitch
- Build karma first, drop links later
- Be genuinely helpful—sales follow naturally

### Product Hunt
- Launch at 12:01 AM PST sharp
- Reply to EVERY comment within 10 minutes
- Don't ask for upvotes publicly (DMs only)

### All Channels
- Track everything (traffic sources, conversions)
- Double down on what works
- Be consistent (30 days minimum before judging)
