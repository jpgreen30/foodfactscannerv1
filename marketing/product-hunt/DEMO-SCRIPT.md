## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Required Assets
- [ ] Screen recording of full app flow
- [ ] Stock footage of concerned parent
- [ ] Congressional report screenshot
- [ ] Clean phone mockups
- [ ] Logo animations

### Recording Checklist
- [ ] Close all other apps/windows
- [ ] Use clean browser (no bookmarks visible)
- [ ] 1080p minimum resolution
- [ ] Good lighting if showing face
- [ ] Test audio levels
- [ ] Record in quiet environment

### Editing Tips
- [ ] Add zoom on key actions
- [ ] Use arrows/circles to highlight elements
- [ ] Keep transitions smooth
- [ ] Match music energy to content
- [ ] Add captions (80% watch muted)
- [ ] End with clear CTA

### Tools
- **Screen Recording:** Loom, Screen Studio, or OBS
- **Editing:** Premiere, Final Cut, or CapCut
- **Captions:** Descript or captioneer.com
- **Music:** Artlist, Epidemic Sound, or Uppbeat

### Timeline
- Record: 30 minutes
- Edit: 2-3 hours
- Export: 15 minutes
- Upload: 15 minutes
- **Total: ~4 hours**
